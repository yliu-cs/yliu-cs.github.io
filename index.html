👨‍💻 Biography

I am a final-year Master’s student at School of Computer Science & Technology, Soochow University, advised by Prof. Shoushan Li and Assoc. Prof. Dong Zhang. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s degree from School of Software, Henan University, advised by Lec. Zhen Wang.

My research interest lies in the intersection of computer vision (CV) and natural language processing (NLP).

I’m applying Ph.D. position start in the 2024 Fall.
